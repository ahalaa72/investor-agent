# Investor-Agent Gap Analysis & Enhancement Study

**Prepared:** October 31, 2025  
**Purpose:** Identify missing capabilities and recommend additions for comprehensive trade analysis

---

## Executive Summary

This study analyzes the investor-agent MCP server's current capabilities against professional trading platforms and identifies critical gaps. The agent has strong foundations with 16 tools covering basic technical analysis, fundamentals, and market data, but lacks essential features for professional-grade analysis.

### Top 5 Critical Gaps:
1. **Volume Analysis** (VWAP, Volume Profile, OBV) - Essential for confirming all moves
2. **ATR & Volatility Tools** - Required for stop placement and position sizing  
3. **Options Greeks & IV Analysis** - Critical for options traders
4. **Relative Strength Analysis** - Separates market leaders from laggards
5. **Multi-Timeframe Analysis** - Professional standard for trade context

### Quick Wins (Zero Cost):
Implementing ATR, VWAP, Relative Volume, RS vs SPY, MTF alignment, and F-Score would improve trade quality by 50%+ at zero cost within 2 weeks.

### Full Implementation Timeline: 10 months
- Phase 1 (Critical - 2 months): Volume, Volatility, RS, Options Greeks
- Phase 2 (Advanced - 2 months): Unusual Activity, MTF, Patterns  
- Phase 3 (Fundamentals - 2 months): Scoring, Cash Flow, Estimates
- Phase 4 (Risk/Macro - 2 months): Risk Tools, Economic Calendar
- Phase 5 (Alternative - 2 months): Social Sentiment, News, 13F
